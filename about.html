Why we exist

Mighty Mainframe is a community of gamers banded together to participate in tournaments and other events for many different games. Starting off as "Community Craft" we grew from a server with a few people hosting Minecraft UHC's to a super organized community 100 people strong.

Aiming for the future

In the future we hope to provide access to simple fun tournaments of many different games to a large audience. The more people we can provide to, the more events we can host!
